Brain Frequency Waves

  • Alpha Waves: Associated with relaxed wakefulness and light meditation, ranging from 8 to 13 Hz.
  • Beta Waves: Indicative of active thinking, problem-solving, and concentration, with frequencies between 14 to 30 Hz.
  • Theta Waves: Occur during deep meditation, creativity, and REM sleep, oscillating at 4 to 7 Hz.
  • Delta Waves: Predominant during deep sleep, healing, and regeneration, typically between 0.5 to 3 Hz.
